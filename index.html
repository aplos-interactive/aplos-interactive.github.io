Fun Facts About Canadian History

  • Canada is the second-largest country in the world by total area.
  • The name "Canada" comes from the Huron-Iroquois word "kanata," meaning "village" or "settlement."
  • Ice hockey and lacrosse are considered Canada's national sports.
